About Me

  • I hope to learn web development and coding to further my career, and one day launch my own business.

Interests

  • I've traveled throughout the Americas and Europe to attend metal shows and festivals. Finding new bands and albums is a lot of fun, and it's easy to do using Metal Archives.
  • I love craft beer and the variety of styles we have in the U.S. Fun fact: Asheville, NC, has the highest number of breweries per capita in the country! Burial Beer Co. is my favorite NC brewery.
  • I started a curated Instagram earlier this year during quarantine boredom to combine my interest in metal and craft beer, and share it with people.
  • Gaming is one of my oldest and most favorite hobbies. Been playing Modern Warfare obsessively for this past year; let's squad up in Warzone if you play, too!
